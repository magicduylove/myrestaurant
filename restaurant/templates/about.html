About Us

Based in Japan and Vietnam, Little Japan is a family owned Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from lots of culture especially Japan and have a menu of 12-15 items that rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Little Japan is owned by NGUYEN HUU TRUONG DUY, who live in Japan to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant.

To craft the menu, DUY relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Japan.

Duy also does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Japan dishes to incorporate additional cuisines from the mediterranean region.
